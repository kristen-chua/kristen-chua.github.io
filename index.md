Kristen Chua is an information services analyst analyst, applied mathematician, and doctoral researcher who studies and develops analytics infrastructures to help institutions understand and govern complex information systems. Her work at the California Digital Library focuses on supports access to scholarly resources across a large academic library network, investigating complex authentication, metadata, and systems-integration issues. Her work with large-scale library data, reporting infrastructure, and decision support systems provide support for the University of California system. Her research interests include quantitative methods, reducing barriers to access, and helping individuals achieve outcomes that might otherwise remain out of reach
