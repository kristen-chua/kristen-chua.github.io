Kristen Chua is an applied mathematician, information services analyst, and education researcher who develops analytics infrastructures to help institutions and people make sense of data systems. Her work at the California Digital Library focuses on supports access to scholarly resources across a large academic library network, investigating complex authentication, metadata, and systems-integration issues. Her work with large-scale library data, reporting infrastructure, and decision support systems provide support for the University of California system. 

Areas of Research:
- Statistics
- Causal Inference
- Information Architecture
- Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
- QuantCrit
- History of Higher education
